Why PhoenixGives Exists

Seva — service offered without attachment.

The purpose of PhoenixGives is to cultivate a community rooted in selfless action and compassion. We offer what we have without attachment or expectation, as an expression of seva — service given with sincerity and humility. The focus is never only on what is given, but on the spirit in which it's given: with care, respect, and presence.

Dignity

Every person we serve is not lesser or separate — they are a reflection of the same presence moving through different circumstances. Giving never looks down.

Karma Yoga

Guided by the principle of right action, we strive to give without ego — letting the giving itself be a form of devotion.

Presence

A folded shirt, a quote on old newspaper, a few words meant only for the person receiving it. Small things, given fully.

The Ritual

OneGives. One newspaper. One quote.

It started small — a few pieces of clothing I loved but no longer needed. I take an old newspaper, wrap what I'm giving, and write something on it by hand. Sometimes a quote. Sometimes just: "this is yours, take it freely." Signed, by PhoenixGives.

What began with clothes is growing — books, pens, fresh food, anything used and still useful, anything that might bring someone a little happiness on a Saturday.
