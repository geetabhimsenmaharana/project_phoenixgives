Reach Out

Whether you want to give, or you need something — this is for you.

Right now, PhoenixGift is just one person, one small idea, and a belief that small things done consistently can matter. If you've found your way to this page, I hope you take this with you: you don't owe anyone your generosity. But the world is asking for more people who choose kindness anyway — who share something, even something small, because it might bring someone else a little light. We don't need everything we do to profit us. We need to remember how it feels when someone smiles at what we gave them, without expecting anything back. That feeling — that's the whole point.

How to be part of this

  • 1

    Follow along on Instagram

    See each Saturday's as it happens, and the small notes that go with it.

  • 2

    Send a message

    Have something to give, a story to share, or just want to say hello? A DM or message goes a long way.

  • 3

    If you need something

    This was built so no one has to ask twice. If you or someone you know could use clothes, food, books, or anything we might have to give — reach out. No explanation needed.

  • 4

    Start your own PhoenixGives

    This idea doesn't need to stay in one city or one pair of hands. If you want to start something similar where you are, reach out — happy to share how it started.
